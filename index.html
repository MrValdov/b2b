How the business branding process works

A clear process keeps production efficient and helps customers understand what they need to provide before ordering.

Simple project flow

  • Choose the business category that fits your company.
  • Send your logo, brand colors, contact details, and preferred products.
  • Approve the design proof before production begins.
  • Receive your finished materials through local delivery, pickup, or shipping when available.

What to prepare

  • Your logo file, or a request for logo setup or logo design.
  • Business name, phone number, website, and social media links.
  • Shirt sizes, decal placement, card text, QR destination, and any required disclaimers.
  • Confirmation that you own or have permission to use submitted artwork.
